ZOLT Privacy Policy

ZOLT is a network speed testing app. This Privacy Policy explains how ZOLT handles data when you run tests, view results, and manage the app.

What the app does not do

This project does not require an account, does not sell personal information, and does not include advertising or analytics SDKs.

What happens when you run a speed test

Running a test sends network traffic to the infrastructure used to power the test, including Measurement Lab for server discovery and Cloudflare for download and upload traffic. Those providers necessarily receive technical request data such as IP address, user agent, timing, and network route information so the test can complete.

What is stored on your device

ZOLT stores test history, local settings, sound preferences, and privacy acknowledgement on the device. This data is used to display charts, summaries, and app behavior. In this codebase, it is not transmitted back to the app publisher.

Retention and deletion

History stays on-device until you delete it or until your selected retention window automatically removes older results. You can clear history and reset local app data from the Settings screen.

Security

Test traffic uses encrypted HTTPS and WSS connections where supported by the provider endpoints. Local app data relies on the operating system sandbox and the security controls of your device.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the support contact listed on the Google Play store page for ZOLT.

ZOLT Data Practices

This page is meant to help keep the app’s Google Play Data safety declaration aligned with the app’s real behavior.

Stored locally on-device

  • Speed test results and timestamps
  • Theme choice and measurement preferences
  • Sound and haptic settings
  • Privacy acknowledgement state

Sent off-device during tests

  • IP address and connection metadata required by speed test providers
  • Upload and download payload traffic generated to measure throughput
  • Latency timing information needed to complete the test

Not used in this project

  • No account creation
  • No ads or analytics SDKs
  • No exact location permission
  • No contact, camera, or microphone access
